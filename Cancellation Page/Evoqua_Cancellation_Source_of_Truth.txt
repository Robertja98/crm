Evoqua Cancellation Source of Truth
====================================

1. Purpose
----------
Provide customers with a clear understanding of Evoqua SDI contract terms and an easy way to generate a compliant cancellation letter.

2. Evoqua Contract Key Points
-----------------------------
- Notice Period Rules:
  * End of Term: Notice must be given not sooner than 3 months and not later than 1 month before the end of the initial or renewal term.
  * Material Breach: 30-day cure period before termination.
- Customer Obligations:
  * Continue payment if required.
  * Return Evoqua-owned equipment.
  * Settle outstanding invoices.
